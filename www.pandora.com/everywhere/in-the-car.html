Take your Pandora stations along for the ride

When you create a Pandora account, your stations are accessible to you wherever you go.

Ask your local dealer or check here to see if Pandora can be found in your car's dash:


Pandora may only be available with optional equipment. Model availability subject to change.

Pandora can be controlled directly through the dash of nearly 200 models. If you don't have one of these models, you can still listen to Pandora in your car by connecting your mobile device via AUX cord or Bluetooth.

Aftermarket Partners

Pandora can also be controlled from select aftermarket devices.
